Your profile settings are where you control your role in Paceday and a few core personal details. You can find them by going to **Settings > Profile** in the left sidebar.

## Switching between Individual contributor and Manager mode

Paceday has two modes designed for different needs:

**Individual contributor** is the default. It gives you focus time protection, habits scheduling, meeting briefs, buffer time, and scheduling links — everything you need to manage your own calendar well.

**Manager** adds the full team layer on top: the My Team sidebar, 1:1 cadences, team focus analytics, no-meeting zones, and the Find a time tool for groups.

To switch:
1. Go to **Settings > Profile**.
2. Find the **Role** section.
3. Click **Manager** or **Individual contributor** to select your role.
4. Click **Save**.

The change takes effect immediately. If you switch to Manager, the **My Team** section appears in your sidebar right away.

[Screenshot: Settings > Profile with the Role section showing two options: Individual contributor and Manager]

## What is preserved when you switch modes

Switching roles does not reset or delete any of your data. Everything carries over:

- **Team list and cadence settings** — if you've been in Manager mode before, your team members and 1:1 frequency settings are waiting for you when you switch back.
- **Habits** — your scheduled habits and their time windows are unchanged.
- **Scheduling links** — any links you've created stay active and shareable.
- **Buffer time and focus settings** — all personal calendar preferences remain in place.

## When to use each mode

Use **Individual contributor** if you don't directly manage anyone, or if you simply want to keep your experience streamlined without the team features in the sidebar.

Use **Manager** if you have direct reports and want visibility into your team's availability, focus trends, and meeting cadences. You can run team analytics and scheduled 1:1s from one place.

If your role changes — for example, you start managing a team or step back from a management role — just update this setting and Paceday adjusts accordingly.
