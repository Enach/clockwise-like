Paceday's primary notification is the **daily morning recap**: a Slack message that gives you a heads-up on your day before it starts. Here's how to set it up and make it your own.

## Requirements

The daily recap is delivered via Slack, so you'll need to connect your Slack account first. If you haven't done that yet, go to **Settings > Integrations > Slack** and click **Connect**.

## Configure the daily recap

1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**.
2. Make sure the **Daily morning recap** toggle is turned on.
3. Set your preferred **send time** — this is in your local timezone. Most people choose something between 7:00 AM and 9:00 AM, so the message arrives before the workday starts.
4. Choose where it goes:
   - **DM** — sends to yourself as a direct message in Slack (the most common option).
   - **Channel** — sends to a specific Slack channel you pick from the dropdown. Useful if your team has a shared standup or daily check-in channel.
5. Click **Save**.

## What the recap includes

Each morning recap contains:
- **Upcoming meetings** for the day, with a one-line brief for each
- **Focus blocks** — any protected focus time you have scheduled
- **Habits** that are scheduled for today

The recap covers your current calendar day from midnight to midnight in your local timezone.

## Preview with "Send test message"

Before committing to a send time, click **Send test message** at the bottom of the Notifications settings page. Paceday sends a sample recap to your configured destination right away, so you can see exactly what it looks like — formatting, content, and all — before the real thing goes out tomorrow morning.

## The Slack preview panel

Just above the Send test message button, you'll see a **Slack preview panel** that shows a rendered mockup of what your recap message looks like based on your current settings. This updates live as you change the configuration, so you can see the effect of your choices without sending anything.

## Disable the daily recap

If you'd rather not receive the morning recap, toggle off **Daily morning recap** at the top of the Notifications settings page and click **Save**. You can re-enable it at any time.
