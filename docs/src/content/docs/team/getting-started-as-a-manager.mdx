title: Getting started as a manager

If you lead a team, Paceday has a dedicated manager experience that surfaces your team's availability, focus trends, and scheduling gaps — all in one place. Here's how to turn it on and get set up.

## Enable manager mode

You can turn on manager mode in two ways:

1. **During onboarding** — when you first sign up, Paceday asks whether you're an individual contributor or a manager. Select **Manager** and you're done.
2. **After onboarding** — go to **Settings > Profile**, find the **Role** section, and switch from **Individual contributor** to **Manager**. Click **Save**.

Once enabled, a **My Team** section appears in the left sidebar.

[Screenshot: Settings > Profile page with the Role toggle showing "Manager" selected]

## What changes when you switch to manager mode

- **My Team** appears in the sidebar, giving you access to your team roster, focus analytics, 1:1 cadences, and the Find a time tool.
- The **Team analytics** tab shows focus and meeting time trends across your team.
- The **No-meeting zones** panel lets you set shared protected hours for the whole team.

Your individual settings — habits, scheduling links, focus blocks, and buffer time — stay exactly as they were.

## Auto-detection: Paceday finds your team for you

When you first enable manager mode, Paceday scans your calendar for the last two weeks and looks for recurring one-on-one meetings (meetings with exactly two attendees that appear more than once). Anyone who shows up in those meetings is automatically added to your **My Team** list.

This usually takes a few seconds. You'll see the results as soon as you open **My Team** in the sidebar.

[Screenshot: My Team list with auto-detected team members]

## Add or remove team members manually

Auto-detection covers most cases, but you can adjust your team list at any time.

**To add someone:**
1. Open **My Team** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Add team member**.
3. Type their name or email address and select them from the list.
4. Click **Add**.

**To remove someone:**
1. Find the person in your **My Team** list.
2. Click the three-dot menu next to their name.
3. Select **Remove from team**.

Removing someone from your team list does not affect their Paceday account or your calendar events with them.
