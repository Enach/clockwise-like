---
title: Creating and editing events
description: How to create, edit, and delete calendar events in Paceday.
---

You can create and manage events directly in Paceday without switching to Google Calendar or Outlook. Any changes you make sync back to your connected calendar automatically.

## Creating a new event

There are two ways to start a new event:

- **Click on a time slot** in the calendar — click and drag to set the duration, then release. A new event drawer opens on the right.
- **Click the + button** in the top-right corner of the calendar. The drawer opens with the current time pre-filled.

[Screenshot: Event creation drawer open on the right side of the screen, with empty fields for title, time, and attendees]

## Filling in the event details

Once the drawer is open, fill in the fields that apply to your event:

1. **Title** — type what the meeting or event is about. Keep it clear enough that attendees will understand it from their calendar.
2. **Date and time** — click the date or time fields to adjust them. You can also change the duration by editing the end time.
3. **Attendees** — start typing a name or email address. Paceday suggests people from your organization's directory. Add as many as you need.
4. **Conferencing** — if you add at least one attendee, a conferencing option appears. Choose **Zoom**, **Google Meet**, or **Microsoft Teams** and Paceday generates the link automatically and adds it to the invite.
5. **Location or room** — type a room name or physical location. If your organization has room calendars set up, Paceday will show availability as you type.
6. **Description** — add an agenda, notes, or a pre-read link here. Attendees see this in their calendar invite.

When you're happy with the details, click **Save**. The event is created in your connected calendar and appears in the Paceday calendar view immediately.

## Editing an existing event

1. Click the event in the calendar view.
2. In the summary panel that opens, click **Edit**.
3. The event drawer opens with the current details filled in.
4. Make your changes and click **Save**.

If the event is a recurring series, Paceday will ask whether you want to edit just this occurrence or all future events in the series.

## Deleting an event

1. Click the event to open the summary panel.
2. Click the **Delete** button (shown as a trash icon).
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Deleted events are removed from your connected calendar and cannot be recovered from Paceday. If you delete by mistake, you can restore the event directly in Google Calendar or Outlook.
