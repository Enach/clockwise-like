Connecting Slack lets Paceday do two things: pull in conversation context that makes your meeting briefs richer, and send you a personalized daily recap each morning so you start the day knowing exactly what's ahead.

## Connect Slack

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**.
2. Find the **Slack** card and click **Connect**.
3. You'll be redirected to Slack's authorization page. Sign in if needed and click **Allow**.
4. Choose whether you want to receive your daily recap as a **direct message** to yourself or in a specific **channel**.
5. Click **Finish** to complete the setup.

[Screenshot: Settings > Integrations page with the Slack card showing a Connect button]

## What Paceday reads from Slack

Paceday reads recent Slack messages to provide context in your meeting briefs — things like relevant discussions that happened before a meeting so you walk in prepared.

Specifically, Paceday looks at:
- Messages from the **last 30 days**
- Only in **channels you're already a member of** and your direct messages
- Messages related to **meeting attendees and event titles** (Paceday matches names and topics, not raw keyword searches)

Paceday does not read every message in every channel. It only surfaces content relevant to your upcoming meetings.

## The daily morning recap

Every morning, Paceday sends you a recap message in Slack. It includes:
- Your **upcoming meetings** for the day with a quick brief for each
- Your **focus blocks** so you know when you've protected time for deep work
- Any **habits** scheduled for today

**To configure the recap:**
1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**.
2. Set the **send time** (for example, 8:00 AM in your local timezone).
3. Choose **DM** (sent to yourself) or pick a Slack **channel**.
4. Click **Save**.

You can also click **Send test message** to preview what your recap looks like before it goes live.

## Disconnect Slack

1. Go to **Settings > Integrations**.
2. Click the **Slack** card.
3. Click **Disconnect**.

Disconnecting removes Paceday's access to your Slack workspace. Your daily recap will stop, and meeting briefs will no longer include Slack context. You can reconnect at any time.
