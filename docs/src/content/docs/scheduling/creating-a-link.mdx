You can create as many scheduling links as you need — one for quick 15-minute chats, another for longer discovery calls, and so on. Each link has its own settings, so you stay in control of what gets booked and when.

## How to create a link

1. Click **Scheduling** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **New link** in the top-right corner.
3. Fill in the fields below.
4. Click **Create link**.

Your link is live immediately.

[Screenshot: The scheduling link creation form showing the name field, duration options, availability settings, buffer toggles, and custom slug input]

## Fields explained

**Name** — A label for your own reference, like "30-min intro call" or "Customer onboarding." This also appears as the meeting title when someone books.

**Duration options** — Choose which lengths bookers can pick from: 15 minutes, 30 minutes, or 60 minutes. You can offer more than one option at the same time — the booker selects their preferred length on your booking page.

**Available days and hours** — Set which days of the week and which hours of those days you're open for bookings. For example, you might allow bookings only Monday through Thursday, between 10:00 and 17:00.

**Buffer settings** — Add a buffer before or after booked meetings, just like the focus-time buffers. This prevents someone from booking right up against another meeting you have.

**Custom slug** — The slug is the last part of your booking URL. By default Paceday generates a random one, but you can change it to something memorable, like `nicolas-30min`. Your full URL would then look like `paceday.app/book/nicolas-30min`. Keep it short and easy to type.

## Sharing your link

Once the link is created, tap **Copy link** to copy the URL to your clipboard. You can paste it anywhere — an email, your Slack status, your email signature, or a website contact page.

There is no account or sign-in required for the person booking. They just open the URL, pick a time, enter their name and email, and confirm.
