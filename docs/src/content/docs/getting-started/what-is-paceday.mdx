title: What is Paceday?
description: An introduction to Paceday and who it's designed for.
---

Paceday is an AI-powered calendar assistant that helps you take back control of your workday. It protects the time you need to focus, helps you build better work habits, and — if you manage a team — gives you clear visibility into how your team's week is actually shaped.

## Who is Paceday for?

**Individual contributors** who feel like their calendar is happening *to* them rather than *for* them. If you spend more time in meetings than doing real work, Paceday automatically carves out focus blocks and guards them from being booked over.

**Managers** who want to support their team without resorting to status meetings. Paceday surfaces a team view so you can see at a glance who has breathing room and who is overloaded — without asking anyone.

## What does Paceday actually do?

- Automatically finds and holds focus time in your week before others can book it
- Tracks habits like deep work, exercise breaks, or end-of-day wrap-ups and nudges you when you fall behind
- Shows meeting prep briefs before your calls so you walk in ready
- Gives managers a live team calendar view with workload signals

## Nothing to migrate

Paceday connects to the calendar you already use — Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook. Your existing events, invites, and recurring meetings stay exactly where they are. Paceday reads what's there and works around it. You do not create a second calendar or move anything.
