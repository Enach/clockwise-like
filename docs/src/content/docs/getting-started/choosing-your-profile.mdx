---
title: Choosing your profile
description: The difference between Individual contributor and Manager mode in Paceday.
---

When you first connect your calendar, Paceday asks you to pick a profile. This is not a permanent decision — you can change it any time — but picking the right one from the start means your sidebar and features will match how you actually work.

## Individual contributor

Choose this if you are an individual contributor, a specialist, or anyone whose primary goal is protecting personal focus time and building better work habits.

With this profile you get:

- **Focus time** — Paceday automatically finds open slots in your week and holds them as focus blocks before others can book over them.
- **Habits** — track recurring personal commitments like deep work sessions, end-of-day wrap-ups, or exercise breaks.
- **Meeting prep briefs** — a short summary appears before each meeting you organize, pulling in relevant context from connected tools.
- **Your calendar** — a clean week and day view showing all your events with color coding.

The team view is not shown in this profile because it's built for managers and would just add noise for individual contributors.

## Manager

Choose this if you lead a team and want visibility into how your team's week is shaped alongside your own calendar.

With this profile you get everything in the Individual contributor profile, plus:

- **Team view** — see your direct reports' calendars side by side, with visual signals for who is overloaded or has meeting-free stretches.
- **Workload indicators** — color-coded signals that surface which team members are meeting-heavy this week.
- **Team habits dashboard** — see aggregate habit completion across your team (individual details stay private).

## How to change your profile later

You can switch profiles at any time without losing any data.

1. Go to **Settings** (click your avatar in the bottom-left corner).
2. Open the **Profile** tab.
3. Click the profile you want to switch to.
4. Click **Save**. The sidebar updates immediately.
