---
title: Habits not scheduling
description: Understand why a habit might not appear on your calendar and how to adjust your settings so Paceday can find a slot.
---

Paceday schedules habits automatically by finding open slots within the time window you've set. If a habit isn't showing up on your calendar, it usually means Paceday couldn't find a suitable gap — not that something is broken. Here's how to figure out what's happening and fix it.

## Common reasons a habit doesn't get scheduled

**Your time window is too narrow and meetings are filling it**
If you set a habit to schedule between 7:00 AM and 9:00 AM, but you have meetings during most of that window, there's no gap left for the habit. Paceday only places habits in genuinely free time within the window you've defined.

**All selected days have back-to-back meetings**
If every day you've selected for the habit is packed end-to-end with meetings, there's nowhere to fit it. Paceday won't interrupt a meeting or squeeze a habit into a gap smaller than the habit's duration.

**The habit duration is longer than the largest available gap**
For example, if your longest free block during the window is 20 minutes but your habit is set to 30 minutes, Paceday won't schedule it — it needs a contiguous block of the full duration.

## Fixes to try

**Widen the time window**
Go to the habit settings and expand the window. For example, change 7:00–9:00 AM to 7:00–11:00 AM. The wider the window, the more opportunities Paceday has to find a slot.

**Add more days**
If the habit is set to Monday, Wednesday, Friday and those days are full, try adding Tuesday or Thursday. Paceday will find the best open day within the expanded set.

**Shorten the habit duration**
If a 30-minute block is hard to find, consider whether 20 or 25 minutes would still be useful. Shorter habits are easier to fit into a busy calendar.

**Reduce meeting density with compression**
If your calendar is consistently back-to-back, Paceday's meeting compression feature can help consolidate meetings to create larger open blocks. Check the **Scheduling** section of your settings to see if compression is enabled.
